Passport

FRESH PASSPORT:

  • 10th Certificate (for Date of Birth
  • 02. Address Proof
    • Election Identity Card
    • Electricity Bill (current & last (1 year back) )
    • Water Supply Bill (current & last (1 year back) )
    • Bank Statement (last 1 year running
  • Four Color Photograph (having white background

Note: if female Applicant is married then they have to submit joint Photograph.

RE-ISSUE OR RENEWAL OF PASSPORT:

01. Photo- copy of Previous Passport (first two pages & last two pages) 02. Address Proof (a) Election Identity Card (b) Electricity Bill (current & last (1 year back) ) (c) Water Supply Bill (current & last (1 year back) ) (d) Bank Statement (last 1 year running) 03. Four Color Photograph (having white background)

Note: If Female/Male Applicant is married then they have to submit joint Photograph.)

MINOR / CHILD CASE(Fresh Passport/ Reissue Passport):

01.Date of Birth (only for fresh passport) 02. Mother/Father Passport or Application receipt 03. If Mother or Father have not issued passport, they have to give NOC 04. Four Color Photograph (having white background) 05. Declaration of Parent/Guardian for Minor Passports (Annexure "H")

TATKAL

A) 10th Certificate B) Three address Proof ( 2 with photo) and 3 photographs

MISCELLANEOUS SERVICES:

01. Address Change (a) Election Identity Card (b) Electricity Bill (current & last (1 year back) ) (c) Water Supply Bill (current & last (1 year back) ) (d) Bank Statement (last 1 year running) (e) Single Photograph

02. Add Spouse's Name (a) Joint Photograph (b) Marriage Certificate (d) Single Photograph

03. Emigration Check Not Required (ECNR) Stamp (a) 10th Certificate (b) Graduation Degree or Mark-Sheets (c) 3 years ITR + PAN Card (d) Single Photograph

04. Police clearance certificate (a) Single Photograph
